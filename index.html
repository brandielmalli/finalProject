About Us

The Boston Bijou Theater opened on Washington Street in 1882 as the first American playhouse lit entirely by electricity. Flash forward 129 years later and Bijou Nightclub is now open for business as a luxury nightlife location and leader in electronic dance and hip hop music. Private Parties – Bijou’s distinct floors offer a versatile landscape for a variety of private functions. Each room’s rich gold or black palette respectively can act as the focal point of an event or as a chameleon to a transformed setting. With full-service catering capacity in our state of the art kitchen, we can prepare everything after parties to a formal sit down dinging experience at our neighboring restaurant, The Ghost Walks. Additionally, our staff, servers and security are available so you can focus on the guest list. Come by and check out Boston’s latest hot spot located at 51 Stuart st, Boston MA 02116

Bottle Service

Bottle Service is the ultimate VIP service to indulge in at Bijou Nightclub. The benefits begin well before your first “cheers” though. First off, the service will gain you and your group the quickest possible entry into the venue, without having to wait on line. The purchase of Bottle Service means that your entire group’s entry to the club is complimentary, so that you can focus your purchases on bottles for the table! By making a Bottle Service reservation, you’ll be making a group reservation wherein you commit to a purchase of bottles of alcohol before you come in, so that the party can be ready and waiting when you arrive.
